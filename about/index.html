About

I'm an iOS Software Engineer who loves doing things right. Nothing excites me more than an opportunity to work on a product which is well designed, well engineered and run by people who care about their craft.

I started working on iOS apps in early 2014 and didn't look back. Since then I had an opportunity to work on multiple products of various scales, from MVPs to full-fledged enterprise solutions.

I'm passionate about building mobile applications and I focus on learning new things about iOS or the Swift Programming Language every single day.

Interests 👨🏻‍🎓

I'm interested in further work on Apple platforms, not only iOS but also macOS/watchOS/tvOS. Other than that, I'm open for learning Server-Side Swift with Vapor and other technologies such as TypeScript, Node or C#.

I have a soft spot for functional programming and I'm interested in doing some Haskell/F# as side projects to improve my skills in Functional Swift. I'm also okay with reactive programming, but I don't consider it a must.

I don't do web development, React Native, Flutter or Android.
