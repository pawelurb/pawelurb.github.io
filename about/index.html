About

I'm a Software Engineer who loves doing things right. Nothing excites me more than an opportunity to work on a product which is well designed, well engineered and run by people who care about their craft.

My primary specialization is building apps for Apple platforms (mostly iOS), something I started in early 2014. Since then I had an opportunity to work on multiple products of various scale, from MVPs to full-fledged enterprise solutions.

I'm passionate about building amazing products and I focus on learning new things about iOS or the Swift Programming Language every single day. Other than that I'm on my way to learn another stack in the near future.
